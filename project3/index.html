About the Artist

Bobbi Stella combines technical mastery and emotional depth to create a sound that is both intimate and epic. Born in Chicago with proud Italian-American roots, Bobbi Stella channels her cultural legacy and personal journey into one artistic mission: transforming vulnerability into power.
